Hi, I'm Neslihan. I was born in Linz, Upper Austria, in 1991. At school, math was my favorite subject because I enjoyed finding solutions to problems. It was like a game to me. As soon as I found a solution, I won the game and like many people are reluctant to lose, I worked on the various tasks until I found a solution. When the evidence for mathematical formulas came into the school material, I decided to study math.

In 2010 I wrote my first source code in C at the Technical University of Vienna. Then I learned many other programming languages ​​until I found my favorites: HTML, CSS and Javascript. Since then I have continued my path as a frontend developer.

Creativity is one of the main reasons I chose frontend. I use it not only for website design, but also for my poems and stories. And when I don't hit the keys, I hit the drums.
